My First Post !!!

  • Jun 15, 2025
  • Categories: Blog
  • Tags: #scioly #introductory #plans

A journey ends, another begins.

This is my first post on this website. The 2025 Science Olympiad Season has concluded. I ended this year (which is my entire Division B career, since I started late), with 10 medals across invitationals, regionals, states, and even won a national silver in the Reach for the Stars event. All-in-all, not a shabby showing. I'm currently considering multiple different ventures headed into my freshman year of high school. I've not yet fully decided what I'm going to do, but this site will be the first place that any updates on that will show up.
